ASII Continuity Pilot

The Continuity Pilot is a controlled, human-reviewed implementation track for one workflow, one team, and one defined case category.

Pilot structure

4–6 week cycle with clear scope boundaries and review checkpoints.

Review model

Analyst and MLRO review remain central; ASII supports evidence assembly and continuity quality.

Outcome

Operationally test case continuity quality before wider institutional rollout.

Workflow

1. Ingest fragmented signals

Travel Rule, sanctions, adverse media, crypto-risk, and case events.

2. Structure case continuity

Normalize evidence and chronology into consistent investigative frames.

3. Analyst / MLRO review

Human reviewers validate logic, evidence completeness, and escalation basis.

4. Reporting readiness

Produce reviewable case continuity outputs for internal governance and regulator dialogue.

Operating principles

Human-reviewed decisions

ASII does not make autonomous compliance decisions.

Evidence-supported continuity

All outputs are built for traceability and explainable reviewer judgment.

Institutional control

Deployment is designed to align with each institution's governance and assurance model.
